Inkwell Literary Magazine
Inkwell exists to provide writers in the Bob Jones University community a place of artistic collaboration and review, and a platform to share their works. The publication works to provide those desiring to pursue a career in creative writing with opportunities to work with a literary publication as writers and editors. They produce quality creative work including short stories, poetry, and creative non-fiction on a monthly basis.
